### What we're building

A lead lands. LeadFlux calls it — from a real SIM, on a real Android phone, in under five seconds. One click on a laptop. No app for the rep to open. No delay for the lead to feel.

LeadFlux is not a CRM. It doesn't store your data long-term or replace your pipeline. It is not a cloud dialer — there's no VoIP number, no moment where the call feels different to the person picking up. It is the execution layer underneath your existing stack: the reflex arc between a lead arriving and a phone ringing.

Built for sales teams of 5–50 reps in India — coaching institutes, edtech admissions, real estate, insurance, and loan agencies — where speed-to-lead decides who wins the deal before either side finishes a sentence.

### Why public repos

Most of LeadFlux — the routing engine, the Android caller service, the AI scoring models — is closed. What we open source is the layer developers touch: SDKs, webhook tooling, our documentation, and the roadmap itself.
